Precursor Signals 预警信号集

Value Bands

session_create max_consec_sleep token_burn_first10pct
session_create 0-1 healthy
2-3 caution
max_consec_sleep <5 healthy

token_burn_first10pct low (<5K) cheap
5K-30K standard

Three early-warning signals that often correlate with later failure: too many sessions, sleep storms, and front-loaded token burn.

At a Glance

FieldValue
Metric IDM43
EnglishPrecursor Signals

MethodAuto / Rule (composite)
Value rangeMultiple sub-fields, see Output Keys
DirectionAll three sub-signals: lower = healthier

Formula

M43_session_create_count = count(action_type == 'create_session')
M43_max_consecutive_sleep = longest run of {sleep, check_session_idle}
M43_token_burn_rate_first10pct = mean tokens per step over the first ⌊0.1 × n_react⌋ steps

Required Inputs

action.action_type, response.usage.total_tokens

Procedure

  1. Count 'create_session' actions.
  2. Compute max consecutive run of {sleep, check_session_idle}.
  3. If n_react ≥ 10, take first ⌊0.1·n_react⌋ steps and compute mean total_tokens per step.

Worked Example

session_create=3 (steps 127, 171, 202), max_consecutive_sleep=7 (steps 69-83), token_burn_first10pct ≈ 15.4K tokens/step.

Edge Cases

  • n_react < 10 ⇒ token_burn_first10pct = None.
  • These signals are strongest as features in a downstream classifier — single-signal thresholds are coarse.

Output Keys

  • M43_session_create_count
  • M43_max_consecutive_sleep
  • M43_token_burn_rate_first10pct
