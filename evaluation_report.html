Key Findings

Core insights from 15,720 evaluations

Low Discrimination Metrics J60/J65: std too low in automated benchmarks (no real interaction). J63/J64: insufficient data (n<200). J47: ceiling effect (mean=4.13). Consider removing from automated-only evaluation.
Dimension Coverage Robustness has 7 metrics (most), Reasoning has 6, Efficiency 4, Behavioral 3, Interaction 3, Overall Quality 1 (J32). Consider adding more Overall/Interaction metrics for balance.
评测方法论分层 (Evaluation Methodology Tiers) Layer 1 — Auto (7): J25/J30/J37/J42/J47/J48/J49 可从轨迹数据直接计算，零成本、可复现，适合大规模筛选。
Layer 2 — LLM Judge (15): 需语义理解，成本中等(~$0.02/eval)，适合精细评估。
Layer 3 — Human (2): J32(整体满意度)/J35(根因归因)/J64(不确定性校准) 需领域专家标注ground truth，成本最高但最可信。
建议：Auto指标做第一轮过滤，LLM Judge做深度评估，Human标注做最终校准与论文报告。

Methodology Validation: Inter-Rater Reliability

Two-Step evaluation (Facts → Score) validated with ICC(2,1) across 3 judge models × 20 trajectories

Two-Step Evaluation Protocol

Step 1 — Factual Inventory (single model)
List all relevant events objectively: what happened, relevance level, utilization status. No scoring.

Step 2 — Mechanical Scoring (3 independent judges)
Given the same fact list, apply quantitative rules (ratios, counts, thresholds) to assign 1-5 score.

Key insight: Separating fact-finding from judgment eliminates the main source of inter-judge disagreement.
